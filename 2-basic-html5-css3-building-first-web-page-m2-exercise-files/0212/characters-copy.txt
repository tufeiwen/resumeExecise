
<h4>Jim Hawkins</h4>
<p>Jim is the narrator of the story and is around years of age. Though goodhearted and competent, he uncovers the plan for a pirate mutiny making rash decisions but also displaying courage. </p>

<h4>”Long John” Silver</h4>
<p>A tavern-keeper in Bristol; the ship's cook; and another pirate that has a wooden leg and a pet parrot called “Captain Flint” on his shoulder. Silver haunts Jim's memories after the adventure.</p>

<h4>Ben Gunn</h4>
<p>Ben Gunn The “man of the island,” and a reformed pirate. He has been marooned on Treasure Island for three years.</p>
